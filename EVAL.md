## Analysis

### RAG Pipeline Advantages

The RAG pipeline outperformed Structured primarily due to:

1. **Richer Context**: Retrieved chunks provide more detail
2. **Natural Language**: Better captures tone and positioning
3. **Flexibility**: Handles varied data formats

### Structured Pipeline Advantages

Structured performed better in:

1. **Data Accuracy**: Type-validated information
2. **Consistency**: Same data produces same output
3. **Traceability**: Clear provenance for every claim

## Conclusion

For private equity diligence, both pipelines offer value:

- **Structured Pipeline**: Best for factual accuracy and investment decisions
- **RAG Pipeline**: Best for initial research and qualitative analysis

**Recommendation**: Use Structured as primary, RAG for supplementary context.
