### ## Company Overview
Runway ("Runway AI") is a New York, NY–based AI video technology company founded in 2018, specializing in tools that enhance creativity through artificial intelligence. The company operates in the audiovisual content creation space, with a focus on making AI accessible and empowering for users. Runway has offices in New York, San Francisco, Seattle, London, and Tel Aviv, and supports a remote workforce globally. The leadership team includes Cristóbal Valenzuela, who is prominently featured in various press releases.

### ## Business Model and GTM
Runway sells AI-driven video and image generation tools to creative professionals and enterprises, utilizing a subscription-based pricing model. Key products include the Runway AI platform, which offers features like video editing, virtual staging, and character performance. The company has established partnerships with notable organizations such as Lionsgate and Getty Images to enhance its product offerings and market reach.

### ## Funding & Investor Profile
On June 29, 2023, Runway announced a $141M funding round led by notable investors, bringing the total raised to $326.5M. Previous funding rounds include a $50M Series C on December 5, 2022, a $35M Series B on December 15, 2021, and an $8.5M Series A on December 16, 2020. Valuation details remain undisclosed.
