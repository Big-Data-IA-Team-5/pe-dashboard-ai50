## Company Overview
Snorkel AI ("Snorkel") is a Redwood City, CA–based AI company founded in 2015, specializing in data-centric AI solutions. The company focuses on programmatic data development to enable enterprises to build specialized AI models using their proprietary data. Snorkel has collaborated with industry leaders such as Google, Apple, and DARPA, and is recognized for its innovative approach to overcoming data labeling bottlenecks.

## Business Model and GTM
The company sells its AI data development platform and services to enterprises, targeting data scientists and subject matter experts. Snorkel's offerings include Expert Data-as-a-Service, which provides specialized datasets, and custom AI/ML solutions for model training and evaluation. Key integrations include partnerships with major tech firms and research institutions, enhancing its market reach and service capabilities.
