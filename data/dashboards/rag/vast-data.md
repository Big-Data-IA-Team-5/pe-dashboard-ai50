## Company Overview
Vast Data ("VAST") is a New York, NY–based AI infrastructure company founded in 2016, operating in the data management and storage categories. Led by CEO Renen Hallak, the company has established itself as a leader in providing innovative solutions for AI workloads. Related companies include Pure Storage and Snowflake.

## Business Model and GTM
The company sells its VAST AI Operating System to data-intensive enterprises, including over 25% of the Fortune 100, with a focus on transforming data infrastructure for AI applications. Products include the VAST AI OS (a unified platform for data management) and DASE architecture (Distributed and Shared Everything), which enhances performance and scalability for AI workloads. Partnerships with NVIDIA bolster its technological capabilities.
