## Company Overview
Runway AI is a New York, USA–based company founded in 2018, specializing in AI, video production, and content creation. The company is led by Founder and CEO Cristóbal Valenzuela. Runway AI operates in a competitive landscape, focusing on innovative solutions for creative professionals and content creators.

## Business Model and GTM
Runway AI targets creative professionals and businesses looking for advanced tools in video production and content creation. The company offers a suite of products including Runway Aleph, a project focused on general-purpose multimodal simulators of the world; Gen-4, an AI model designed for video and image generation; and the Runway API, which provides programmable features for creative tools. The pricing model for these products has not been disclosed, nor are there any listed integration partners or reference customers.
