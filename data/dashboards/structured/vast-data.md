## Company Overview
VAST Data is a New York-based data storage company founded in 2016. The company specializes in providing innovative storage solutions that unify and orchestrate storage, database, and application runtime environments. VAST Data is led by Founder & CEO Renen Hallak, Co-Founder & Chief R&D Officer Shachar Fienblit, Co-Founder Jeff Denworth, President Michael Wing, CFO Amy Shapero, CMO Marianne Budnik, CRO Rick Scurfield, Co-Founder & VP of Technology Alon Horev, Chief Architect Asaf Levy, VP of Human Resources Karmit Shitrit, and Field CTO Andy Pernsteiner. The company operates in a competitive landscape with notable players in the data storage sector.

## Business Model and GTM
VAST Data sells its flagship product, the VAST AI Operating System, which provides a comprehensive operating environment for artificial intelligence applications. This system allows AI to sense, learn, reason, and act globally, thereby enhancing operational efficiency for its users. The company targets enterprises looking for advanced data storage solutions but does not disclose specific pricing models or tiers. Currently, there are no publicly listed integration partners or reference customers.
